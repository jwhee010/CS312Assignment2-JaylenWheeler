The upgrade: Epsilon 3A

How's it better?

Compared to the standard Epsilon 3, the Epsilon 3A has the 100% accurate digital charge display. This charge display removes any potential worry caused by uncertainty. While the standard version gives a certain range for charge, the 3A gives an exact number.
